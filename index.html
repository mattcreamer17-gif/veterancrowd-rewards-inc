You built a card-linked loyalty platform on top of Visa's authorization stream, patent pending. The member side verifies through the VA or a .mil address. How we would read your stack

Most startups in a platform like yours pour AI budget into the layer where Postgres and a few well-written rules would do the job faster, cheaper, and with better guarantees. We would help you name which is which before you spend a dollar on the wrong layer.

60%

Traditional code and database work

Merchant dashboards, reporting, onboarding automation, eligibility state. Postgres, API work, clean admin surfaces. The boring layer that decides whether second-year merchants renew.

30%

Rule-based logic

Eligibility cascade, .mil plus VA plus sponsor flows, reward tier rules, merchant-funded reward math. Rules that should be legible to a human and auditable by Visa.

10%

Genuinely an AI problem

Fraud-pattern detection on verification, content categorization for the podcast and network side, a merchant-onboarding copilot for the sales conversation. Small, specific, scoped.
